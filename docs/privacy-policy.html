Sinag - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Sinag ("the App") is a free solar calculator tool for homeowners in Davao City, Philippines. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Camera and Photos: The App uses your device camera to photograph your electric bill. Images are processed entirely on your device using on-device OCR (optical character recognition). Bill photos are never uploaded to our servers.

Bill Data: Extracted data (kWh consumed, billing period, generation charge, total amount) is used solely to calculate your solar estimate. This data is sent to our server only to perform the calculation and is not stored permanently.

Email Address (Optional): If you choose to email a copy of your estimate, we temporarily store your email address and estimate data for up to 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not create user accounts or require login
  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your location (solar irradiance data is based on the Davao City region generally, not your specific location)
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or ad tracking
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools

3. Third-Party Services

Google ML Kit (On-Device): The App uses Google ML Kit for text recognition. This processing happens entirely on your device. No image data is sent to Google. See Google ML Kit Terms.

NASA POWER API: The App retrieves publicly available solar irradiance data for the Davao City region from NASA's POWER API. No personal data is sent with these requests.

4. Data Storage and Security

Bill images remain on your device and are not transmitted. Calculation requests are processed via encrypted HTTPS connections. Optional email estimates are stored securely and automatically purged after 90 days.

5. Permissions

  • Camera: To photograph your electric bill for OCR scanning
  • Internet: To send calculation requests to our server and retrieve solar data
  • Storage/Photos: To allow selecting a bill photo from your gallery

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
